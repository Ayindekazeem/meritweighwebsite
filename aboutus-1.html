About Us

More than 100 weighbridge installations in Nigeria

Meritweigh Nigeria Limited was established in 2015 and is one of the fastest growing Supplier of Digital Truck Weighing Systems in Nigeria. As a team, we have been in the weighing business for the last 5 Years. Being a privately held company, we have the ability to change and adapt quickly to our customer's requirement of weighing products & solutions than our competition.

Meritweigh Nigeria Limited supplies a wide range of truck weighbridges of various capacitiesand other Digital weighing equipment and is committed to provide total solutions to digital Weighing equipment users. With more than 100 weighbridge installations in Nigeria (number is growing steadily). MERITWEIGH has a reputation of being a prompt team right from quotations, through delivery and service of weighbridges. Personalized service, convenience, confidence and quality are the few important factors our customers like in our organization and keep repeating the orders for their requirement of weighing products.

Our commitment is reflected in the relationship we have built with our team and our customers. Quality of our products are customer’s driven.

We continuously strive to be better quality company in the hearts and minds of our customers with our creative problem solving and continuous improvement policies. We serve our valued customers to their entire satisfaction with our quality products and prompt services as we firmly believe in CUSTOMER SATISFACTION.

We Supplies truck weighing systems of capacity ranging from 10T to 200T of platform size varying from 6 x 3 Meters to 18 x 3 Meters. Nevertheless we are too glad to manufacture any standard sizes and capacity of weighing systems in line with our company policy of “meeting customers’ requirements by creative problem solving”.
